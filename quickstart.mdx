This guide walks you through everything you need to go from a blank workspace to a fully configured HR environment. By the end, you'll have a live organization profile, at least one department, your first employee record, and a processed payroll run — all in under 15 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    1. Open your browser and navigate to [app.agentlize.com](https://app.agentlize.com).
    2. Click **Get Started Free** on the landing page.
    3. Enter your **work email address** and choose a strong password, then click **Create Account**.

       <Note>
         Use your company email address rather than a personal one. Agentlize HRM uses your email domain to help you identify colleagues who sign up later and to enforce single sign-on (SSO) policies if you configure them.
       </Note>

    4. Check your inbox for a verification email from `no-reply@agentlize.com`. Open the email and click **Verify Email Address**.
    5. You'll be redirected back to the app and prompted to begin setting up your organization.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up your organization">
    After verifying your email, the **Organization Setup** wizard launches automatically. Fill in the following fields:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Company Name** | The legal or trading name of your business. This appears on payslips and reports. |
    | **Timezone** | Select the primary timezone for your headquarters. Attendance records and payroll cut-off dates are calculated relative to this timezone. |
    | **Currency** | Choose your default payroll currency (e.g., USD, EUR, GBP). You can add additional currencies later for multi-currency payroll. |
    | **Fiscal Year Start** | Select the month your financial year begins (e.g., January for a Jan–Dec cycle, April for an Apr–Mar cycle). |

    Click **Save and Continue** when you're done. You can edit all of these settings later under **Settings → Organization Profile**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure departments">
    Departments group employees for reporting, leave approvals, and payroll runs. You need at least one department before you can add employees.

    1. On the **Departments** setup screen, click **Add Department**.
    2. Enter a department name (e.g., *Engineering*, *Sales*, *Human Resources*).
    3. Optionally assign a **Department Head** — you can come back to this once you've added employees.
    4. Click **Save Department**.
    5. Repeat for as many departments as you need, then click **Continue**.

    <Tip>
      Before you start adding employees, invite any other HR admins or workspace administrators to your account. Go to **Settings → Team Members → Invite Member** and enter their work email. Collaborating from the start means you're not the only person who can approve payroll or manage leave policies.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your first employee">
    With your organization and at least one department in place, you're ready to create your first employee record.

    1. Navigate to **Employees → All Employees** in the left sidebar.
    2. Click **Add Employee** in the top-right corner.
    3. Fill in the required fields:

       | Field | Notes |
       |---|---|
       | **Full Name** | Enter the employee's legal name as it should appear on payslips. |
       | **Work Email** | This is used to send the employee their account invitation. |
       | **Department** | Select from the departments you created in Step 3. |
       | **Start Date** | The employee's official employment start date. |
       | **Salary** | Enter the gross annual or monthly salary and select the pay frequency. |

    4. Click **Save Employee**. Agentlize HRM automatically sends the employee a welcome email with a link to activate their self-service account.

    You can add further details — job title, address, emergency contacts, documents — from the employee's profile page at any time.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run your first payroll">
    With at least one active employee on record, you can process your first payroll run.

    1. Navigate to **Payroll → Payroll Runs** in the left sidebar.
    2. Click **New Payroll Run**.
    3. Select the **pay period month** you want to process (e.g., *January 2025*).
    4. Agentlize HRM pre-populates salary figures, attendance-based deductions, and any approved leave without pay. Review each line item for accuracy.
    5. If everything looks correct, click **Approve Payroll**. You'll be asked to confirm before the run is finalized.
    6. Once approved, payslips are automatically generated and made available to each employee in their self-service portal.

    <Note>
      For a detailed walkthrough of payroll configuration — including tax tables, deduction rules, and bulk imports — see the [Payroll feature guide](/features/payroll).
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's next?

Now that your workspace is live, here are the recommended next steps:

- **[Account Setup](/getting-started/account-setup)** — Add your company logo, configure compliance settings, and fine-tune your organization profile.
- **[Roles & Permissions](/getting-started/roles-permissions)** — Understand what each role can do and assign the right access level to every team member.
- **[Employee Management](/features/employee-management)** — Bulk-import existing employees, configure custom profile fields, and set up onboarding checklists.
- **[Leave Management](/features/leave-management)** — Define leave types, accrual rules, and approval workflows before employees start submitting requests.
