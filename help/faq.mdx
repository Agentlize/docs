## Account & Access

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I reset my password?">
    If you have forgotten your password or are locked out of your account:

    1. Go to the Agentlize HRM login page.
    2. Click **Forgot Password?** below the sign-in form.
    3. Enter the email address associated with your account and click **Send Reset Link**.
    4. Check your inbox for a password reset email (also check your spam/junk folder). The link expires after **1 hour**.
    5. Click the link, enter your new password, and confirm it.

    If you do not receive the email within a few minutes, verify that you are using the correct email address, or contact your HR Admin to confirm your account is active.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I have multiple HR Admins?">
    Yes — Agentlize HRM supports an **unlimited number of HR Admin users** on all plans. There is no cap on how many people can have the HR Admin role.

    To grant HR Admin access to a user, go to **Settings → Team Members**, find the user, click **Edit Role**, and select **HR Admin**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I transfer ownership of the workspace?">
    Workspace ownership can only be transferred by the current **Owner** of the workspace. Because this is a sensitive operation that affects billing, SSO configuration, and data access, it must be completed with the assistance of the Agentlize HRM support team.

    To initiate a transfer, the current Owner should email **support@agentlize.com** with the subject line "Workspace Ownership Transfer" and include:
    - Your workspace name
    - The email address of the current Owner
    - The email address of the new Owner

    Our support team will verify both parties and complete the transfer within one business day.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many employees can I add?">
    The number of employees you can add depends on your **subscription plan**:

    - **Starter**: up to 25 employees
    - **Growth**: up to 100 employees
    - **Business**: up to 500 employees
    - **Enterprise**: unlimited employees

    To view your current plan and employee count, go to **Settings → Billing**. If you are approaching your limit or need to upgrade, click **Upgrade Plan** from the same page, or contact your account manager.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Employee Management

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I import employees from a spreadsheet?">
    Yes. You can bulk-import employees from a CSV file rather than adding them one by one.

    1. Go to **Employees** in the left sidebar.
    2. Click the **Import** button in the top-right corner.
    3. Download the **CSV template** provided — this ensures your data is in the correct column format.
    4. Fill in the template with your employee data and save it.
    5. Upload the completed CSV file and click **Preview Import**.
    6. Review the preview for any validation errors (e.g., missing required fields or duplicate email addresses).
    7. Click **Confirm Import** to create all valid employee records.

    The import supports up to **500 employees per file**. For larger imports, split the data into multiple files.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I archive an employee who leaves the company?">
    When an employee leaves, you should set their status to **Terminated** rather than deleting their profile. This preserves all historical records — payslips, leave history, attendance, and documents — for compliance and audit purposes.

    To terminate an employee:

    1. Open the employee's profile from **Employees**.
    2. Click **Actions → Terminate Employee**.
    3. Enter the **Employment End Date** and select a **Termination Reason** (e.g., Resignation, Redundancy, End of Contract).
    4. Click **Confirm Termination**.

    The employee's profile is archived and removed from active employee counts and payroll. Their historical data remains fully accessible under **Employees → Archived**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can employees update their own information?">
    Yes. Employees have access to a **self-service portal** where they can update certain information themselves, without needing to go through HR:

    - **Personal contact details** — phone number, personal email address, home address
    - **Bank account information** — for payroll direct deposit (subject to re-verification)
    - **Emergency contacts** — name, relationship, phone number
    - **Profile photo**

    Sensitive fields such as job title, department, salary, and start date can only be updated by an HR Admin or Manager with the appropriate permissions.

    Employees access the self-service portal by clicking their **avatar (top-right corner) → My Profile**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Payroll

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I run payroll and it has an error?">
    Once a payroll run is **approved**, it cannot be undone or reversed. This is by design to maintain a complete and tamper-proof payroll audit trail.

    If you discover an error after approval, you should run a **correction payroll**:

    1. Go to **Payroll → New Payroll Run**.
    2. Select **Correction Run** as the type.
    3. Select the pay period you need to correct and the affected employees.
    4. Adjust the salary components as needed to account for the over- or under-payment.
    5. Approve the correction run — it will be recorded separately and labeled as a correction in your reports.

    If the error is significant, contact support@agentlize.com for guidance on how to handle the correction in your accounting system.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I run payroll for a subset of employees?">
    Yes. When creating a new payroll run, you can filter and include only the employees you want to process:

    - **By department**: select one or more departments to include only those employees
    - **By individual**: manually select specific employees from the employee list
    - **By employment type**: filter for full-time, part-time, or contract employees

    This is useful when you need to run an off-cycle payroll for a specific group — for example, when onboarding a new team member mid-month or processing a correction for a single department.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is payroll data encrypted?">
    Yes. Agentlize HRM encrypts all payroll and employee data:

    - **At rest**: all data stored in our databases is encrypted using AES-256 encryption.
    - **In transit**: all data transmitted between your browser (or our mobile app) and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
    - **Payslips**: individually generated payslip PDFs are stored in encrypted object storage with access controls limiting who can download them.

    Agentlize HRM is SOC 2 Type II certified and complies with GDPR data protection requirements.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Leave

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can employees take a negative leave balance?">
    This is configurable per leave type. By default, employees cannot submit leave requests that exceed their available balance. However, you can enable **negative balance (advance leave)** for specific leave types.

    To configure this setting:

    1. Go to **Settings → Leave Policies**.
    2. Select the leave type you want to modify (e.g., Annual Leave).
    3. Toggle **Allow Negative Balance** on or off.

    When enabled, employees can submit leave requests beyond their current balance, resulting in a negative balance that will be offset by future accruals. You can optionally set a **minimum balance cap** (e.g., maximum of -5 days) to limit how far negative the balance can go.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to unused leave when an employee is terminated?">
    The treatment of unused leave at termination is configurable per leave type and may also depend on your local employment law requirements:

    - **Paid out**: the remaining balance is calculated at the employee's daily rate and added as an additional payment in the final payroll run.
    - **Forfeited**: unused balance is zeroed out without payment.

    To configure this, go to **Settings → Leave Policies**, select the leave type, and look for the **Termination Balance Treatment** setting.

    We strongly recommend consulting with your HR or legal advisor to ensure your configuration complies with the employment legislation in your jurisdiction before finalizing this setting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I set different leave policies for different departments?">
    Yes. Agentlize HRM supports department-level leave policy assignment. This means you can create different leave policies (with different accrual rates, entitlements, or rules) and apply them to specific departments.

    For example, you might offer a more generous annual leave entitlement to employees in your Sales department, or apply different rules for your remote team.

    To assign a leave policy to a department:

    1. Go to **Settings → Leave Policies** and create or select a policy.
    2. Under **Policy Assignment**, select **Specific Departments**.
    3. Choose the departments to which this policy should apply.

    Employees inherit the leave policy of their assigned department. If an employee is moved to a different department, their leave policy will update to match the new department's policy at the start of the next leave period.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Attendance

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if an employee forgets to clock out?">
    If an employee forgets to clock out at the end of their shift, their shift record will show an open check-in with no corresponding check-out. This prevents the attendance record from being automatically approved.

    A manager or HR Admin can manually correct the record:

    1. Go to **Attendance** in the left sidebar.
    2. Use the filters to find the employee and the affected date.
    3. Click the attendance record that shows the missing clock-out.
    4. Click **Edit Record** and enter the correct clock-out time.
    5. Add a note explaining the manual correction (e.g., "Employee forgot to clock out — clock-out time confirmed with employee").
    6. Click **Save**. The correction is logged in the record's audit trail.

    <Note>Employees can also submit a correction request from their own self-service portal, which a manager can then review and approve.</Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Agentlize HRM support biometric attendance?">
    Yes. Agentlize HRM supports integration with biometric attendance hardware devices (fingerprint scanners, facial recognition terminals, and RFID card readers) via our hardware integration module.

    Because hardware setup is specific to the device model and your network configuration, biometric integrations are set up with the assistance of our technical team. To get started, contact **support@agentlize.com** with details about the hardware you use and the number of locations you need to connect.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I view attendance from a mobile device?">
    Yes. The **Agentlize HRM mobile app** provides full attendance functionality on both iOS and Android:

    - **Employees** can clock in and clock out, view their own attendance history, and submit correction requests.
    - **Managers** can view their team's attendance in real time, approve or reject correction requests, and receive attendance anomaly alerts.
    - **GPS check-in** is supported — you can require employees to be within a defined geofence radius of the workplace to clock in.

    Download the app from the **App Store** (iOS) or **Google Play** (Android) and sign in with your Agentlize HRM credentials.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
