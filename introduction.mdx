## What is Agentlize HRM?

Agentlize HRM is an AI-powered Human Resource Management platform built to automate and simplify every stage of the employee lifecycle. From the moment a candidate enters your recruitment pipeline to the day an employee's final payslip is processed, Agentlize HRM centralizes your HR operations in one place — eliminating spreadsheets, reducing manual errors, and freeing your HR team to focus on people rather than paperwork.

The platform is organized around six core modules:

- **Employee Management** — Maintain a single source of truth for every employee's profile, documents, contracts, and employment history.
- **Attendance Tracking** — Record clock-ins and clock-outs, manage shifts, and get real-time visibility into who is present, late, or absent.
- **Payroll Processing** — Automate salary calculations, tax deductions, bonuses, and reimbursements, then generate payslips with one click.
- **Leave Management** — Define leave policies, let employees apply for time off, and route approvals through configurable manager workflows.
- **Recruitment** — Post job openings, track applicants through a visual pipeline, schedule interviews, and convert successful candidates into employees.
- **Performance Evaluation** — Run structured review cycles, set goals, collect 360° feedback, and surface actionable insights with AI-assisted summaries.

**Who is Agentlize HRM for?**

| Persona | How they use Agentlize HRM |
|---|---|
| **HR Teams** | Manage the full employee lifecycle, run payroll, enforce compliance, and generate reports. |
| **Managers** | Approve leave requests, review team attendance, conduct performance evaluations, and track team goals. |
| **Employees** | View payslips, apply for leave, update personal information, and track their own attendance and performance. |

Whether you're a startup hiring your first ten employees or a mid-market company with hundreds of staff across multiple departments, Agentlize HRM scales to fit your organization's structure.
