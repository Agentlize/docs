---

Agentlize HRM's performance module lets you run structured, transparent review cycles entirely within the platform — from setting up the review form and inviting participants to collecting self-assessments, completing manager evaluations, and sharing final results with employees. This guide walks through a typical **annual review cycle** end to end, covering the responsibilities of HR Admins, employees, and managers at each stage.

---

## For HR Admins: setting up the review cycle

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new review cycle">
    Go to **Performance > Review Cycles** in the main sidebar and click **Create Cycle** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure cycle details">
    Fill in the core cycle settings:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Cycle Name** | A clear, identifiable name — for example, `Annual Review 2025` or `Mid-Year Review H1 2025` |
    | **Start Date** | The date the cycle opens and employees can begin completing self-assessments |
    | **End Date** | The deadline by which all manager reviews must be submitted |
    | **Review Type** | Choose from **Annual**, **Mid-Year**, **Probation**, or **Project-Based** |

    Click **Next** to continue.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select participants">
    Choose who is included in this review cycle:

    - **All Active Employees** — includes every employee currently in the system
    - **Specific Departments** — select one or more departments from the list
    - **Custom List** — search for and add individual employees manually

    Participants are split automatically into **reviewees** (those being reviewed) and **reviewers** (their direct managers). You can adjust reviewer assignments manually on the next screen if any reporting lines need to be overridden for this cycle.

    Click **Next**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure the review form">
    Build the form that reviewers and employees will complete:

    1. Click **Add Section** to create form sections. Common sections include:
       - *Goal Achievement* — employees and managers rate progress against set objectives
       - *Core Competencies* — ratings on skills such as communication, collaboration, and problem-solving
       - *Open-Ended Questions* — text fields for qualitative feedback (e.g., "What were your key achievements this period?" or "What support do you need from your manager?")
       - *Overall Rating* — a summary score on a scale you define (e.g., 1–5 or Exceeds Expectations / Meets Expectations / Needs Improvement)

    2. For each section, configure the **rating scale** (numerical, descriptive labels, or stars), whether the section is **required**, and whether it is **visible to the employee** in their final shared results.

    3. Click **Preview Form** to see exactly what employees and managers will see before you launch.

    Click **Next** when the form is ready.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable self-assessment">
    Toggle on **Enable Employee Self-Assessment** to allow employees to complete their own review before their manager does.

    Set a **Self-Assessment Deadline** — this should be a few days before the manager review deadline to give managers time to read employee submissions before completing their own ratings.

    Enabling self-assessment gives managers valuable context and tends to produce more balanced, discussion-ready reviews.

    Click **Next**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Launch the cycle">
    Review your cycle summary — cycle name, dates, participant count, and form structure. If everything looks correct, click **Launch**.

    Agentlize HRM immediately:

    - Sets the cycle status to **Active**
    - Sends an **email notification** to all participants with a direct link to their review task
    - Creates individual review records for each employee–manager pair in the cycle

    You can monitor progress and send reminders from the cycle dashboard at any time after launch.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## For employees: completing your self-assessment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access your review">
    When the review cycle launches, you will receive an email notification with a direct link to your self-assessment form. You can also access it at any time by going to **Performance > My Reviews** in your self-service portal and clicking the active review cycle.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete each section of the form">
    Work through each section of the review form:

    - **Goal Progress** — for each goal listed, select your achievement rating and write a brief description of what you accomplished, what you learned, and any obstacles you faced.
    - **Competency Ratings** — rate yourself honestly on each listed competency using the scale provided. Add a comment to support each rating — specific examples carry the most weight.
    - **Open-Ended Questions** — take time with these. Thoughtful, specific answers here give your manager useful context and help drive a more productive review conversation.

    You can **save your progress** at any point and return to finish later — your answers are not submitted until you click the final submit button.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit before the deadline">
    Once all required sections are complete, click **Submit Self-Assessment**. You will see a confirmation screen and receive a confirmation email.

    <Warning>
      Self-assessments **cannot be edited after submission**. Review your answers carefully before clicking Submit. If you need to make a correction after submitting, contact your HR Admin to request a reopen.
    </Warning>

    After submission, your answers are made visible to your manager as they complete their review of you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## For managers: completing team reviews

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your team's reviews">
    Go to **Performance > Team Reviews**. You will see a list of all your direct reports who are participants in the active cycle, along with a status indicator for each (Not Started / In Progress / Submitted).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete each direct report's review">
    Click an employee's name to open their review. You will see two panels side by side:

    - **Left panel** — the employee's self-assessment responses (if self-assessment is enabled and they have submitted)
    - **Right panel** — your manager review form

    For each section:

    1. Read the employee's self-assessment response.
    2. Enter your own rating and comments in the manager review panel. Where your assessment differs from the employee's self-rating, add a specific explanation.
    3. In the **Development Comments** section, note areas for growth, recommended training, or goals for the next period. Be constructive and specific.

    Take time with each review — the quality of your written feedback significantly affects how useful employees find the process.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit the review">
    When you have completed all sections for an employee, click **Submit Review**. Repeat for each direct report.

    You can return to a saved review at any time before the cycle end date to make edits. Once submitted, reviews are locked unless an HR Admin reopens them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## For HR Admins: closing the cycle and sharing results

<Steps>
  <Step title="Monitor completion progress">
    Go to **Performance > Review Cycles** and click into the active cycle. The **Progress** tab shows a real-time breakdown of:

    - Self-assessments submitted vs. pending
    - Manager reviews submitted vs. pending
    - Overall completion percentage

    You can filter the view by department to identify where bottlenecks are occurring.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send reminders to incomplete reviewers">
    For any manager or employee who has not yet submitted, click **Send Reminder** next to their name. Agentlize HRM sends them a personalized email reminder with a direct link to their pending review task.

    To send bulk reminders to everyone who is still pending, click **Send All Reminders** at the top of the Progress tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Close the cycle">
    Once all reviews are submitted (or the cycle end date has passed), click **Close Cycle**. This locks all reviews and prevents any further edits.

    You will be asked to confirm the close action. Closed cycles remain accessible as read-only records in Performance > Review Cycles.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Share results with employees">
    After closing the cycle, decide which results to make visible to employees:

    1. In the cycle settings, toggle on **Share Results with Employees**.
    2. Choose what to share: **Manager Review Only**, **Both Self-Assessment and Manager Review**, or a **Custom Selection** of specific sections.
    3. Click **Publish Results**.

    Each employee immediately receives an email notification and can view their results in **Performance > My Reviews** in their self-service portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Employees can only see their **own** final review results. They cannot view the ratings or comments given to any of their peers, regardless of whether they share the same manager or department.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Before publishing results, use the **Calibration View** to compare ratings across departments and normalize scores. Go to **Performance > Cycle > Calibration** to see a distribution chart of all ratings in the cycle. This helps you identify inflated or deflated ratings in specific teams and align standards across the organization before employees see their results.
</Tip>
