Onboarding a new hire in Agentlize HRM takes you through every step of the process in one place — from building the employee's profile and configuring their compensation to uploading documents, assigning shifts, and sending their login credentials. Following this guide end-to-end ensures your new employee is fully set up and ready to work on day one. Estimated time: **~10 minutes** per employee.

<Info>
  **Prerequisites**

  Before you begin, make sure the following are in place:
  - You have an **HR Admin** or **Manager** role in Agentlize HRM.
  - The employee's **department** has already been created (Settings > Departments).
  - The signed **offer letter** is ready to upload.
</Info>

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the employee profile">
    Navigate to **Employees** in the main sidebar, then click **Add Employee** in the top-right corner.

    Fill in the following fields in the *Personal Information* section:

    | Field | Details |
    |---|---|
    | **Full Name** | Employee's legal first and last name |
    | **Personal Email** | Used to deliver the login invitation — must be an address the employee can access before they start |
    | **Work Email** | The company email address (can be added now or updated after provisioning) |
    | **Department** | Select from your existing departments |
    | **Job Title** | The role as it will appear on payslips and org charts |
    | **Manager** | Select the employee's direct line manager |
    | **Employment Type** | Choose **Full-time**, **Part-time**, or **Contract** |
    | **Start Date** | The employee's first official working day |

    Click **Save & Continue** to move to the next section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set compensation">
    On the *Compensation* tab, enter the employee's pay details:

    - **Base Salary** — enter the agreed annual or monthly salary figure.
    - **Pay Frequency** — choose **Monthly**, **Bi-weekly**, or **Weekly** to match your payroll cycle.
    - **Currency** — select the currency in which the employee is paid.
    - **Allowances** — click **Add Allowance** to attach any recurring extras such as a housing allowance, transport allowance, or meal subsidy. Give each allowance a name, amount, and taxability status.

    Click **Save & Continue** when done.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload documents">
    Open the **Documents** tab on the employee's profile.

    Click **Upload Document** and attach each required file. Common documents to upload at onboarding include:

    - Signed employment contract
    - Government-issued ID copy
    - Tax declaration or withholding forms
    - Any role-specific certifications or credentials

    For each upload, select the appropriate **Document Type** from the dropdown and set a **Expiry Date** where applicable (e.g., for ID copies or work permits). Documents are stored securely and are only accessible to HR Admins and the employee themselves.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign shifts">
    Navigate to **Attendance** in the main sidebar, then select **Shifts**.

    Find the relevant shift schedule for the employee's role and hours, then click **Assign Employees**. Search for the new employee by name, select them, and click **Assign**.

    If no suitable shift schedule exists yet, click **Create Shift** first to define the working hours, break times, and applicable days before assigning.

    Shift assignment determines how the employee's attendance will be tracked and how overtime is calculated — so confirm this is correct before the employee's first day.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure leave entitlements">
    Return to the employee's profile and open the **Leave** tab.

    Agentlize HRM automatically applies the leave policy associated with the employee's department. Verify that the correct policy is shown under *Applied Policy*. If it is incorrect, click **Change Policy** to assign the right one.

    If the employee is joining **mid-year**, their leave entitlement may need to be prorated:

    1. Click **Adjust Balance** next to the relevant leave type.
    2. Enter the prorated number of days in the **Amount** field.
    3. Add a brief note in the **Reason** field (e.g., "Joining mid-year — prorated annual leave").
    4. Click **Save**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send the welcome invitation">
    With the profile complete, navigate to the **Overview** tab of the employee's profile.

    Click the **Send Invitation** button. This triggers an automated welcome email to the employee's **personal email address** containing:

    - Their login credentials (username and a temporary password)
    - A direct link to the **Employee Self-Service Portal**
    - A summary of their first-day information

    The employee will be prompted to set a new password on first login.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify access">
    On the employee's start date (or shortly before), confirm that onboarding is complete:

    1. Open the employee's profile and check the **Status** badge — it should read **Active**.
    2. Ask the employee to confirm they have received the invitation email and successfully logged in.
    3. Verify they can see their dashboard, payslips section, and leave balance in the self-service portal.

    If the employee reports any access issues, you can resend the invitation from the **Overview** tab by clicking **Resend Invitation**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The invitation email is sent from **noreply@agentlize.com**. Ask your new employee to check their **spam or junk folder** if the email doesn't appear in their inbox within a few minutes. You may also want to ask your IT team to whitelist this sender domain in your company's email filters.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Use the **Onboarding Checklist** feature to track completion status across multiple new hires at once. Go to **Settings > Onboarding** to configure your checklist items, then monitor progress for each employee from the Onboarding dashboard. You can assign checklist tasks to specific HR team members to distribute the workload.
</Tip>
